Swoosh Logo

Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight founded Nike on January 25, 1964, as Blue Ribbon Sports (BRS). Upon changing its name to Nike, Inc. on May 30, 1971, the company adopted the Swoosh as its official logo the same year. In today's day some shoes have the backwards swoosh which was started by Dennis Rodman.
